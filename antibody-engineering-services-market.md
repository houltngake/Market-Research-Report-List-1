<p><p>Antibody engineering services encompass several market types that enhance antibody production and functionality. Gene synthesis involves creating specific DNA sequences for desired antibodies. Transient expression allows for rapid antibody production in host cells without stable genetic integration. Isotype switching modifies antibody classes to improve efficacy and reduce immunogenicity. Chimerisation combines parts of different antibodies, enhancing their therapeutic potential. Together, these services facilitate the development of more effective therapeutic antibodies for various medical applications.</p></p>
