<p><p>The aromatherapy machines market includes various diffuser types catering to different preferences. Fan diffusers disperse essential oils using a fan to spread the aroma, providing a light scent. Ultrasonic aromatherapy diffusers utilize water to create a fine mist, blending oils with steam for longer-lasting diffusion and humidification benefits. Nebulizing diffusers break down oils into fine particles without water, delivering concentrated aromas quickly and effectively, making them ideal for therapeutic applications. Each type offers unique features for enhanced aromatherapy experiences.</p></p>
