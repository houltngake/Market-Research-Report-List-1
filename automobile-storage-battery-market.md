<p><p>The automobile storage battery market includes several key types. Nickel Cadmium (NiCd) batteries are known for their durability and cost-effectiveness but suffer from memory effects. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries offer higher energy density and are more environmentally friendly. Lithium-ion batteries dominate due to their lightweight and high energy capacity. Lithium polymer batteries provide flexibility in design and are used in advanced applications. Lead-acid batteries, the traditional choice, are widely used for their low cost and reliable performance, especially in conventional vehicles.</p></p>
