<p><p>The automotive chassis dynamometers market is segmented into single roller and multi-roller systems. Single roller dynamometers consist of one large roller that tests vehicle performance and emissions under static conditions, making them simpler and more cost-effective for lighter vehicles. In contrast, multi-roller dynamometers feature multiple rollers that accommodate a broader range of vehicle types, enabling more accurate performance testing at higher speeds and loads. This diversity allows manufacturers and researchers to choose systems tailored to specific testing requirements.</p></p>
