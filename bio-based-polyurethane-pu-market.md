<p><p>The bio-based polyurethane (PU) market is categorized into three primary types: rigid foams, flexible foams, and CASE (coatings, adhesives, sealants, and elastomers). Rigid foams are primarily used for insulation in construction and refrigeration. Flexible foams are commonly found in furniture, automotive interiors, and mattress production. The CASE segment encompasses a range of applications where bio-based PU offers eco-friendly alternatives for coatings, adhesives, sealants, and elastomers, enhancing sustainability while maintaining performance and durability.</p></p>
