<p><p>The bradycardia market includes various treatment modalities for patients with abnormally slow heart rates. Aminophylline acts as a bronchodilator that may indirectly influence heart rate. Ephedrine and scopolamine provide additional pharmacological options. Advanced interventions such as pacemakers and implantable cardioverter defibrillators offer long-term management solutions by regulating heart rhythm, ensuring effective treatment for bradycardia patients.</p></p>
