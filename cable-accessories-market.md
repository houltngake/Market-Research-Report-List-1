<p><p>The cable accessories market is divided into three main types: underground cables and accessories, submarine cables and accessories, and overhead cables and accessories. Underground cables are designed for direct burial and often require specialized joints and terminations. Submarine cables facilitate underwater communication and power transmission, necessitating waterproof connectors and enhanced protection. Overhead cables, typically used for power distribution and transmission, require insulators, spacers, and other accessories to ensure safety and reliability in exposed environments. Each type addresses specific installation and operational challenges.</p></p>
