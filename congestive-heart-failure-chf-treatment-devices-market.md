<p><p>The Congestive Heart Failure (CHF) treatment devices market includes Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) and Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs). CRT devices improve the heart's efficiency by coordinating contractions in patients with electrical conduction issues. In contrast, ICDs monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks to prevent sudden cardiac arrest in high-risk individuals. Both devices play crucial roles in managing CHF, enhancing patient quality of life, extending survival rates, and addressing complex heart rhythm disorders associated with heart failure.</p></p>
