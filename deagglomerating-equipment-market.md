<p><p>The deagglomerating equipment market is categorized into vertical and horizontal equipment types. Vertical equipment typically employs gravity to enhance the deagglomeration process, effectively breaking apart clumped materials by utilizing vertical motion. Conversely, horizontal equipment utilizes mechanical forces in a lateral orientation, facilitating the disaggregation of particles through shear and impact. Both types serve essential roles across various industries, ensuring consistent material quality by improving flow characteristics and particle size distribution in processes such as pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals.</p></p>
