<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Dental Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Dental Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental scalers market primarily includes magnetostrictive and piezoelectric dental scalers, both crucial for effective dental cleanings. Magnetostrictive scalers utilize ultrasonic vibrations generated by a magnetic field to remove plaque, calculus, and tartar. They operate at a wider range of frequencies and can cover larger surface areas. In contrast, piezoelectric scalers use electrical energy to create ultrasonic vibrations, providing precise control and efficiency, often preferred for delicate procedures. Both types are essential for maintaining oral hygiene and dental health.</p></p>
