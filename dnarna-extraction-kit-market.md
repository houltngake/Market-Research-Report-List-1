<p><p>The DNA/RNA extraction kit market features several specialized types catering to diverse research and diagnostic needs. Cell-free DNA (cfDNA) kits target circulating DNA in bodily fluids, while sequence-specific DNA kits isolate targeted genomic regions. Total RNA kits extract all RNA types, whereas total nucleic acid kits provide comprehensive RNA and DNA extractions. Sequence-specific RNA kits focus on particular RNA sequences, and purified RNA kits offer refined RNA isolations. Viral RNA kits are essential for extracting RNA from viral pathogens, critical for disease research and diagnosis.</p></p>
