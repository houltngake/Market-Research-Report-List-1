<p><p>The dry running rotary vane vacuum pump market is categorized into single-stage and two-stage types. Single-stage pumps create a vacuum in a single operation, making them suitable for applications requiring moderate vacuum levels. In contrast, two-stage pumps utilize two sets of vanes to achieve deeper vacuum levels, enhancing efficiency and performance for demanding applications. Both types are oil-free, ensuring clean operation and reducing maintenance, making them ideal for industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and electronics.</p></p>
