<p><p>Electrically Tunable-Focusing Lenses (ETFL) are advanced optical devices that can dynamically adjust their focal length through electrical input. The market for ETFL includes two primary types: Flat Convex and Flat Concave lenses. Flat Convex lenses are designed to converge light, offering applications in imaging systems and projectors. In contrast, Flat Concave lenses diverge light, making them suitable for beam expansion and optical instrumentation. Both types enhance flexibility in various technologies, enabling precise control in diverse optical applications.</p></p>
