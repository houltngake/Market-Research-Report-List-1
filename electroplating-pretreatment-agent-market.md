<p><p>The electroplating pretreatment agent market is segmented into three main types based on pH levels: acidic, neutral, and alkaline. Acidic agents effectively remove oxides and contaminants, enhancing adhesion for metal plating. Neutral agents maintain a balanced pH, providing versatility for various substrates without aggressive reactions. Alkaline agents, characterized by their higher pH, are effective in removing oils and greases, making them suitable for preparing surfaces of heavily contaminated materials. Each type plays a crucial role in achieving optimal electroplating results.</p></p>
