<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electrostatic Wafer Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic wafer chuck market includes two primary types: Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. </p><p>Coulomb-type chucks utilize traditional electrostatic forces to hold wafers in place, offering simple design and effective performance. In contrast, JR-type chucks leverage the interaction between a dielectric layer and an electric field to enhance wafer grip while minimizing contamination and damage. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, optimizing processes like photolithography and etching by ensuring precise wafer positioning and stability.</p></p>
