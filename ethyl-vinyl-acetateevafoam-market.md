<p><p>The Ethyl Vinyl Acetate (EVA) foam market is categorized into several types based on manufacturing processes. Extrusion molding involves melting EVA pellets and shaping them into continuous sheets or rolls, ideal for packaging and insulation. Injection foaming molding allows for precise, complex shapes by injecting EVA foam into molds, commonly used in automotive and consumer products. Compression molding shapes EVA foam by applying heat and pressure, suitable for producing dense, high-performance parts in sports equipment and footwear applications.</p></p>
