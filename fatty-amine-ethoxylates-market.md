<p><p>The fatty amine ethoxylates market is categorized into three main types based on amine structure. Primary fatty amines are derived from natural sources and exhibit high biodegradability, making them suitable for eco-friendly applications. Secondary fatty amines, characterized by two hydrocarbon chains, offer enhanced stability and performance in various formulations. Tertiary fatty amines, featuring three hydrocarbon chains, are known for their emulsifying properties and are widely used in industrial and consumer products, including detergents and surfactants.</p></p>
