<p><p>The femtosecond laser surgery equipment market consists of two primary types: full femtosecond and half femtosecond systems. Full femtosecond lasers deliver rapid, high-precision pulses for intricate procedures, such as refractive eye surgeries, providing unparalleled accuracy and minimal tissue damage. In contrast, half femtosecond systems combine femtosecond technology with other laser methods, offering a more versatile approach for various surgical applications. Both types enhance surgical outcomes, ensuring safer, more effective interventions across ophthalmology and other medical fields.</p></p>
