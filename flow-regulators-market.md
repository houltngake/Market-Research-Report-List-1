<p><strong>The Flow Regulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Flow</li><li>Mass Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow regulators market is categorized into volumetric flow and mass flow types. Volumetric flow regulators measure the volume of fluid passing through a system, providing precise control for applications like irrigation and industrial processes. In contrast, mass flow regulators focus on the mass of the fluid, offering greater accuracy in applications involving gases and liquids with varying densities, such as chemical processing. Both types are essential for optimizing efficiency and ensuring safety in fluid management systems.</p></p>
