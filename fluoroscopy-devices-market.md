<p><p>Fluoroscopy devices are essential imaging tools that allow real-time visualization of internal structures and functions. The market is segmented into two primary types: fixed fluoroscopes and mobile fluoroscopes. Fixed fluoroscopes are permanently installed in hospitals or clinics, offering high-resolution imaging for various procedures, often favored for their stability and advanced features. In contrast, mobile fluoroscopes are portable units that can be utilized in multiple locations, providing flexibility and convenience for emergency situations or bedside imaging, enhancing access to critical diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
