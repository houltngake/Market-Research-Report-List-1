<p><p>The Functional Polyester Heat Shrinkable (PETG) Film market consists of two primary types: One Way Shrink Film and Two-Way Shrink Film. One Way Shrink Film shrinks predominantly in one direction, making it ideal for applications requiring tight packaging around products. In contrast, Two-Way Shrink Film shrinks both vertically and horizontally, offering enhanced versatility for complex shapes and sizes. Both types are utilized in various industries, including food packaging, consumer goods, and pharmaceuticals, emphasizing durability and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
