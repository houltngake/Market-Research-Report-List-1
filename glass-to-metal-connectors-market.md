<p><ul><li>Matched Connectors/Seals</li><li>Compression Connectors/Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass-to-Metal Connectors market comprises two main types: matched connectors/seals and compression connectors/seals. Matched connectors create a precise, hermetic seal between glass and metal, ensuring reliable performance in high-temperature and high-pressure environments. Compression connectors use a mechanical compression method to bond glass to metal, offering flexibility in design and application. Both types are crucial in industries such as aerospace, telecommunications, and medical devices, where they provide essential durability and protection against environmental challenges.</p></p>
