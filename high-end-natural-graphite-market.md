<p><ul><li>Amorphous Graphite</li><li>Flake Graphite</li><li>Crystalline Vein Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end natural graphite market comprises three main types: amorphous graphite, flake graphite, and crystalline vein graphite. Amorphous graphite, characterized by its non-crystalline structure, is often used in lubricants and batteries. Flake graphite, known for its layered structure and high purity, is ideal for applications in batteries and fuel cells. Crystalline vein graphite, found in metamorphic rocks, boasts exceptional purity and is utilized in specialized applications like electronics and nuclear industries. Together, they cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
