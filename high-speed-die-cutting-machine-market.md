<p><ul><li>Rotary Die Cutting Machines</li><li>Platen Die Cutting Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed die cutting machine market features two primary types: rotary die cutting machines and platen die cutting machines. Rotary die cutters utilize a cylindrical die to cut through materials continuously, making them ideal for high-volume production and intricate designs. In contrast, platen die cutting machines employ a flat die and a pressing mechanism to cut materials in a batch process, providing flexibility for varying sizes and shapes. Both types cater to diverse industries, including packaging, printing, and automotive.</p></p>
