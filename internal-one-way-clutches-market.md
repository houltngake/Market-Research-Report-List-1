<p><ul><li>Roller Design</li><li>Spiral Design</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The internal one-way clutches market consists of two primary designs: roller and spiral. Roller designs utilize cylindrical rollers that engage when rotation occurs in one direction, providing a locking mechanism. This design is known for its efficiency and durability, making it ideal for applications requiring high torque. Spiral designs, on the other hand, use spiral-shaped elements that wedge into place under load, offering smooth engagement and disengagement. Both types cater to varying industrial needs, enhancing performance in machinery and drive systems.</p></p>
