<p><ul><li>Wick Lamp</li><li>Pressure Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kerosene lamp market is primarily divided into two types: wick lamps and pressure lamps. Wick lamps use a simple wick soaked in kerosene to produce light through combustion, making them easy to use and maintain. In contrast, pressure lamps utilize a pressurized system to create a brighter and more efficient flame, often resulting in more intense illumination and longer burn times. Both types serve essential roles in providing lighting in areas lacking electricity, with varying applications based on user needs.</p></p>
