<p><p>Laser Fizeau interferometers are crucial optical instruments used for high-precision measurements in various applications. The market is primarily segmented into homodyne and heterodyne interferometers. Homodyne interferometers utilize a single frequency laser source, providing simplicity and stability, making them suitable for applications like surface profiling. In contrast, heterodyne interferometers employ two different frequencies, allowing for enhanced measurement resolution and dynamic range, which is advantageous for fields like metrology and telecommunications. Both types serve unique requirements across industries.</p></p>
