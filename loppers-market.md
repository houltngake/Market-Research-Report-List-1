<p><p>Loppers are pruning tools used for trimming branches, and they come in two main market types: Anvil Type and Bypass Type. Anvil Loppers feature a straight blade that cuts against a flat surface, making them ideal for dry, dead wood. Bypass Loppers, on the other hand, have curved blades that slide past each other, allowing for cleaner cuts on live wood. The choice between these types often depends on the specific gardening needs and the type of plants being pruned.</p></p>
