<p><p>The medical phased array ultrasound probe market comprises three main types: line type, face type, and ring type. Line type probes utilize a linear arrangement of transducer elements, ideal for vascular and musculoskeletal imaging. Face type probes have a rectangular arrangement for broad imaging applications, offering improved resolution in larger areas. Ring type probes feature a circular formation, enabling 3D imaging and superior detection of structures, particularly in echocardiography. Each type serves specific clinical needs based on imaging requirements.</p></p>
