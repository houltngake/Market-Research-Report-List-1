<p><p>Pendulum Tuned Mass Dampers (PTMDs) are devices used to reduce vibrations in structures, typically caused by wind or seismic activity. They consist of a mass suspended from a pendulum, which absorbs and dissipates energy. The market is divided into two types: Passive PTMDs, which operate without external energy and rely on the natural motion of the pendulum, and Active PTMDs, which utilize sensors and actuators to adaptively respond to dynamic conditions, offering enhanced vibration control.</p></p>
