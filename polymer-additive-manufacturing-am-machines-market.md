<p><p>Polymer Additive Manufacturing (AM) encompasses various technologies, including Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), which uses thermoplastic filaments; Stereolithography (SLA), which utilizes UV light to cure liquid resin; Digital Light Processing (DLP), similar to SLA but employing a digital light projector; Material Jetting (MJ), where droplets of material are deposited layer by layer; and Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), which fuses powdered materials using lasers. Other methods include Binder Jetting and Multi Jet Fusion, each catering to specific applications and materials.</p></p>
