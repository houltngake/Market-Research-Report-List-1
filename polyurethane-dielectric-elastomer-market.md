<p><p>The Polyurethane Dielectric Elastomer market comprises two main types: thermosetting elastomers and thermoplastic elastomers. Thermosetting elastomers are characterized by their curing process, resulting in a rigid, cross-linked structure that offers excellent heat resistance and mechanical stability. In contrast, thermoplastic elastomers are flexible and can be remolded upon heating, providing ease of processing and recyclability. Both types are utilized in applications such as actuators, sensors, and flexible electronics due to their superior dielectric properties and elastic behavior.</p></p>
