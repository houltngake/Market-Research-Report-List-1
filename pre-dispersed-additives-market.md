<p><p>The pre-dispersed additives market comprises various types based on the polymer matrix used. EPDM-based additives enhance thermal stability and weather resistance, making them ideal for automotive and construction applications. SBR-based additives improve abrasion resistance and mechanical properties, typically used in tire manufacturing. NBR-based additives are known for their oil resistance, suitable for seals and gaskets. Natural rubber-based additives offer eco-friendly options with good elasticity. Other categories may include additives tailored for specific applications or unique properties.</p></p>
