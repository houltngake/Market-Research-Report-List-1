<p><p>The Primary Care Electronic Health Record (EHR) market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EHRs are hosted on external servers, offering benefits like remote access, scalability, and lower initial costs. These systems often include automatic updates and data backup, enhancing security and convenience. On-premise EHRs, on the other hand, are installed locally on healthcare providers' servers, providing complete control over data and customization but requiring higher upfront expenses and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
