<p><p>The pure iron powder market consists primarily of two key types: carbonyl iron powder and electrolytic iron powder. Carbonyl iron powder is produced through the thermal decomposition of iron carbonyl, resulting in a high-purity, fine powder with excellent magnetic properties, often used in electronics and soft magnetic applications. Electrolytic iron powder, derived from the electrolysis of iron salts, offers high purity and low oxygen content, making it suitable for applications in metallurgy and advanced manufacturing processes.</p></p>
