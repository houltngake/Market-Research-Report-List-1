<p><p>The Quantitative PCR (qPCR) market is categorized into three types based on throughput capacity. Low throughput qPCR is designed for small-scale experiments, often used in research and diagnostics with fewer samples. Medium throughput qPCR balances capacity and efficiency, suited for labs that handle a moderate number of samples regularly. High throughput qPCR, on the other hand, is optimized for large-scale studies, enabling rapid analysis of numerous samples simultaneously, typically utilized in genomics and clinical applications.</p></p>
