The market is primarily divided into two types: flexible and rigid OCS. Flexible OCS adapts to dynamic conditions, allowing for ease of installation and maintenance, making it suitable for varied terrains. In contrast, rigid OCS offers greater structural stability and longevity, often preferred in high-speed rail systems or areas requiring consistent power delivery. Both types are crucial for efficient rail operations.</p></p>
