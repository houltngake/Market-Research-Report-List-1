<p><p>The self-expandable metal stents market includes three main types: esophageal stents, gastroduodenal stents, and colon stents. Esophageal stents are designed to treat strictures or obstructions in the esophagus, improving swallowing and quality of life. Gastroduodenal stents facilitate food passage between the stomach and duodenum, often used in cases of tumors or blockages. Colon stents are employed to alleviate obstructions in the colon, promoting bowel function. Each stent type is crucial for managing gastrointestinal conditions effectively.</p></p>
