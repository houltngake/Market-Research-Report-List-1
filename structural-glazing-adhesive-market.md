<p><p>The structural glazing adhesive market is segmented into one-component and two-component systems. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, providing ease of application and uniform curing properties. They are ideal for applications where convenience is paramount. In contrast, two-component adhesives require mixing before application, allowing for customizable properties such as increased strength or flexibility. This type is often preferred for high-demand projects where enhanced performance is essential, particularly in structural and load-bearing applications.</p></p>
