<p><ul><li>PTC</li><li>NTC</li><li>CTR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermistor market features three main types: Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) thermistors, Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) thermistors, and Current Temperature Resistor (CTR) thermistors. NTC thermistors decrease resistance as temperature rises, making them ideal for temperature sensing and compensation applications. PTC thermistors increase resistance with rising temperature, commonly used for overcurrent protection and self-regulating heating elements. CTR thermistors offer a reliable method for monitoring current and temperature, enhancing circuit protection and system reliability, thereby catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
