<p><p>The Vehicle Speed Monitoring System market comprises various technologies for tracking vehicle speed. Radar-based systems utilize radio waves to detect speed and distance, offering reliable performance in diverse conditions. Laser-based systems, employing light pulses, provide high precision but may require direct line-of-sight. Ultrasonic-based systems use sound waves to measure distance and speed, suitable for short-range applications. Camera-based monitoring systems leverage video analytics to assess speed using image processing, enhancing surveillance capabilities. Each system type serves different operational needs within traffic enforcement and safety.</p></p>
