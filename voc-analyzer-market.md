<p><strong>The VOC Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC analyzer market is primarily segmented into two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID analyzers utilize ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, providing accurate and rapid measurements suitable for various environmental monitoring applications. In contrast, MOS sensors detect VOCs through changes in resistance caused by the interaction of gases with a metal oxide surface, offering compact, cost-effective solutions for indoor air quality assessments. Both types cater to diverse industrial and research needs.</p></p>
