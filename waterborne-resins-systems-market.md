<p><p>Waterborne resin systems are categorized into high molecular weight and low molecular weight types, each serving specific applications. High molecular weight resins offer enhanced durability, chemical resistance, and glossy finishes, making them ideal for coatings, adhesives, and sealants in demanding environments.
